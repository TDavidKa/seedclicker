HOW-TO CLICK

STEP 1: CLICK

 In the top-left section of the page, you will notice a large seed. This is the first step in your gardening journey, and will allow you to purchase other plants. To begin, simply click the seed. Now, you have collected one seed. Congradulations!

STEP 2: CLICK

 Once you've collected a sufficient number of seeds, you're ready to buy your first plant! Oh, it's called Seeds again? What a creative name. Now you have a steady supply of 0.1 seeds coming to you automatically per second. What an honor! Want more? Buy More!

STEP 3: CLICK

 Look to the top right of the screen. What a pretty little garden. You'll notice that purchasing plants will add them to the garden. Sweet! You can't really interact with them, but feel free to click them as many times as you want.
